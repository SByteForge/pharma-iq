PHARMA REBATE CONTRACT AGREEMENT

Article 1 - Rebate Tiers
Manufacturer agrees to pay rebates based on formulary tier placement.
Tier 1 placement: 15% rebate on net sales.
Tier 2 placement: 8% rebate on net sales.
Tier 3 placement: 3% rebate on net sales.

Article 2 - Market Share Requirements
Rebates are conditional on achieving minimum market share thresholds.
Tier 1 rebate requires minimum 35% market share in therapeutic class.
Tier 2 rebate requires minimum 20% market share in therapeutic class.

Article 3 - True-Up Clause
Quarterly reconciliation will be performed against actual dispensing data.
If market share falls below threshold, rebate reverts to next tier rate.
True-up payments due within 30 days of quarter close.

Article 4 - Compliance and Audit
Manufacturer has right to audit dispensing records annually.
Payer must retain records for minimum 5 years.
